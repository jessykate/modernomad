One Rent, Live Anywhere

In every city around the globe, creative thinkers and practical visionaries are exploring and innovating new approaches to social systems rooted in sharing, collaboration, and connectivity.

The Embassy Network is a global community of people and coliving spaces at the heart of this movement. Membership provides access to hosted stays embedded in comfortable, engaged communities, in locations around the world.

The Embassy Network is for those who seek to immerse themselves with innovative peers and a dynamic environment with events, discussions and projects, rather than staying in impersonal hotels or spare rooms.

Become a Member

The Embassy Network is for creatives and professionals traveling for work and collaboration, modern nomads, and recent transplants.

Our members move seamlessly around the planet. They are ambitious, optimistic, critical creators, actively working towards positive futures. We are currently opening up alpha memberships on a rolling basis.

Add your Space

Coliving represents a wide spectrum of modern shared living spaces exploring new approaches to home and living that emphasize connectivity, collaboration, and mutual support.

Like coworking has done for work, coliving houses act as a platform to support residents and amplify their aims and projects. Listing is free and you set your prices.

A Global Commons

The Embassy Network seeks to create a new form of collaboratively managed commons.

The network takes a 5% fee from memberships to support the development of the community, software platform, and documentation, all of which is open source under CC-BY or the Affero GPL.

How It Works

Apply to become an individual member or to add your space
Add credit to your account in $500 increments and receive a 20% discount at all locations
Stay “standby” and book unused capacity day-of, at cost, for a $100 monthly membership fee
Book your stays online, and find participating social spaces to meet people and attend events
Payments are distributed to member locations based on where you stay
